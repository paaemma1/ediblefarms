About Edible Farms

Founded in the heart of Accra Ghana, Edible Farms started with a simple belief: great food starts with integrity. We raise premium catfish in pristine, natural ponds with zero antibiotics or growth hormones.

Our closed-loop system respects the environment while delivering the buttery, clean taste that chefs and families love. From fingerling to harvest, every step is transparent and responsible.
